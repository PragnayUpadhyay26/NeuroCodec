NeuroCodec

"To develop a deep learning-based image compression system that learns optimal entropy modeling using a hyperprior architecture and achieves better rate–distortion performance than traditional codecs."

Problem Statement

Traditional codecs like JPEG use fixed transforms and handcrafted entropy coding. These methods cannot adapt dynamically to image content. With increasing demand for high-resolution storage and bandwidth efficiency, intelligent compression systems that learn compact representations directly from data are required.

Literature Review

Hyperprior-based neural compression (Ballé et al., 2018) demonstrates improved entropy modeling and superior rate–distortion performance compared to JPEG and JPEG2000.

Research Gap

Classical codecs use static probability models. Our hyperprior network predicts latent mean and variance dynamically, improving compression efficiency.
